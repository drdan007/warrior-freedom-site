THE WARRIOR RULES
01
Never risk more than 2-3% per trade
A single bad trade should never materially impact your portfolio. Size accordingly — always.
02
Set stops before you enter
My rule: -40% stop on all options positions. Decide your exit before emotion takes over.
03
Respect elevated IV environments
When VIX is above 20, reduce size by 50%. The market is telling you something — listen.
04
Diversify across expiries
Never concentrate all positions in the same expiration week. Spread risk across time.
